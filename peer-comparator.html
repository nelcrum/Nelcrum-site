Which communities are statistically your peers?

Pick your county and we match it against 3,000+ U.S. counties on income, need, education, housing, and — unlike older peer tools — its trajectory: how tract incomes have actually moved since 2020. Weight what matters to you. See who else faces your exact situation, and learn what's working there.

Inspired by the Chicago Fed's Peer Cities Identification Tool — extended to every county, with adjustable weights and trend-aware matching.

Your county Peers

Method: counties are compared on z-scored indicators grouped into three weighted dimensions — income & economy (median household income, unemployment, educational attainment), need & distress (poverty, LMI tract share, rent burden), and trajectory (tract income movement 2020–2024). Sources: ACS 2023 5-year; FFIEC tract designations analyzed by NELCRUM. Peer matching is a screening aid, not a formal benchmarking study — ask us for the rigorous version.
