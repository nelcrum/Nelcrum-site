Notable foundations are real organizations. Look any of them up in the Funder Intelligence Report for their actual IRS 990 figures.

The state aggregates shown here are representative estimates anchored to public magnitudes, provided for orientation while we finish building the verified dataset derived from IRS 990-PF filings. They are clearly badged as estimates and should not be cited as exact figures. Notable-foundation names are real, publicly-known organizations headquartered in each state; no dollar figures are attributed to any individual named foundation. For verified per-organization numbers, use the Funder Intelligence Report.
