Is this address in an LMI census tract?

Enter any U.S. street address or 11-digit tract GEOID. Get the tract's CRA income level, LMI eligibility, income trend since 2020, and how it sits against its county and state, in seconds. Built for CRA officers, CDFIs, and economic development agencies.

Income levels computed from tract median family income as a percent of area median (FFIEC methodology, 2024 vintage; 72,542 tracts, 44 states). This is a screening tool: for examinations and formal filings, confirm with the FFIEC Geocoding/Mapping System. Opportunity Zone and NMTC layers are in development.
