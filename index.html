Who we are

A partner that has sat on both sides of the table.

NELCRUM Solutions is a consulting firm that partners with mission-driven organizations to build strategy, capacity, and evidence. Our team brings over a decade of public- and nonprofit-sector experience, and, having worked as both funders and applicants, we understand from the inside what makes an organization sustainable, effective, and fundable.

Our work spans organizational strategy, board governance, program design and evaluation, research design, and qualitative & quantitative analysis, translating rigorous social science into practical, measurable results. Funding readiness is an outcome of stronger organizations, not the starting point.

How we work

From diagnosis to durable capacity.

We don't start with a proposal; we start with evidence. Every engagement moves through the same arc: understand where you really stand, decide what matters most, then build the capacity to sustain it.

01 · Diagnose

See clearly, with evidence

We map where your organization actually stands, through data, candid conversation, and our own diagnostic tools, not assumptions.

02 · Plan

Decide what matters most

We translate findings into a prioritized, realistic roadmap: the few moves that will move the needle, sequenced for your stage and capacity.

03 · Build capacity

Put it into practice

We work alongside your team to strengthen strategy, governance, systems, and skills, so the gains outlast the engagement. Funding readiness follows naturally.
