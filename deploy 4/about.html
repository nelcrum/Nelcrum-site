Who we are

Rigor, translated into results.

NELCRUM Solutions is a consulting firm that partners with mission-driven organizations to build strategy, capacity, and evidence. Our team brings over a decade of public- and nonprofit-sector experience, and, having worked as both funders and applicants, we understand from the inside what makes an organization sustainable, effective, and fundable.

Our work spans organizational strategy, board governance, program design and evaluation, research design, and qualitative & quantitative analysis, translating rigorous social science into practical, measurable results. Increasingly, that work extends into software, building the custom tools that turn good decisions into lasting systems.
