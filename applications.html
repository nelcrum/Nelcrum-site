Why we build

Off-the-shelf software is built for the average organization. Yours isn't average.

Mission-driven organizations bend their work to fit generic tools, or pay for features they will never use. We build the smaller, sharper thing that fits how you actually operate, captures the data you are accountable for, and grows with you.
