How it works
01
Free scoping call

Twenty minutes. You describe the project; we confirm the package fits — or tell you if it doesn't.

02
Agreement & scope

One-page agreement with the deliverable, timeline, and fee in writing. No open-ended billing.

03
Half to start

Fifty percent on signing starts the clock. We handle the data gathering; you answer a short intake.

04
Delivery & debrief

You get the deliverable plus a debrief call. Balance due within 15 days of delivery.

Are fees flexible?

Under certain circumstances, yes — agency size and age matter. If a package is out of reach, say so on the scoping call and we will discuss reasonable alternatives.

Do you guarantee funding?

No — no honest consultant can. Winning grants depends on fit, capacity, relationships, and competition. What we guarantee is the deliverable: verified data, a clear plan, and honest advice about your odds.

Not sure which package?

Book the free scoping call and bring your tool results. We will tell you which package fits — or that you don't need one yet.
