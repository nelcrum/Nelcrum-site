Why isn't the consultation free anymore?

Because it isn't a sales call. You get 90 minutes of real advisory work and a written plan you keep, whether or not you hire us. The free tools remain the no-cost way to explore fit.

Are fees flexible?

Under certain circumstances, yes. Agency size and age matter. If an engagement is out of reach, say so in your consultation and we will discuss reasonable alternatives.

Do you guarantee funding?

No. No honest consultant can. Winning grants depends on fit, capacity, relationships, and competition. What we guarantee is the deliverable: verified data, a clear plan, and honest advice about your odds. For what it is like to work with us, read what our clients say.
