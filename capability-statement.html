Company overview

NELCRUM Solutions, LLC is an evidence-led advisory firm that helps mission-driven organizations make better decisions and build the custom software to act on them. We serve foundations, nonprofits, community development institutions, and government partners, bringing more than a decade of public- and nonprofit-sector experience as both funders and grantees. Our advisory and application-development practices reinforce each other: research informs the tools we build, and those tools scale the insight back into the field.
