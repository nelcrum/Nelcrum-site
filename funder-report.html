Funder Intelligence · Free tool

See what a funder actually funds.

Search by name (partial is fine), 9-digit EIN, or browse by state and program area. We pull public IRS Form 990 filings and summarize giving, assets, and multi-year trend, so you know whether you fit before you spend time applying.

No account needed. Try a sample report.
How it works
01

Public 990 data

Every figure comes from IRS Form 990 filings, the same public returns funders are required to file.

02

Read the trend

Giving, assets, and expenses across years show scale and direction, and whether your ask is in range.

03

Verify at the source

The full report links to every original 990 PDF, so you can confirm the exact grants-paid line yourself.

Data via public IRS filings (through ProPublica's Nonprofit Explorer). Filings typically lag one to two years, and grant-level detail is most complete for private foundations that file Form 990-PF. Figures are a research starting point, not verified financial advice.
